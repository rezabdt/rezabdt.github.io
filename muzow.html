Project Name :
Muzow Music Player - Play Audio MP3

Powerful quick search all music and audio files, Easy to support all music & audio file formats, Custom background skin. Muzow was designed to bring a better experience to the user when they listen to music. Muzow player scans all music automatically and group them by title, artist, album.

I ran this app Google Ads campaign. The main target here was to increase the download of app, so I have brought the download from the countries of the lowest CPC. The campaign budget was only $10 and I have come up with a total of 498 downloads in this $10.
